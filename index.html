Mallorca has many things to do, if want to relax, or eat some delicius seafood, or even go for a hiking. We can do all these things only in one place, Mallorca. Spend some time looking into our options which are divided into three main categories, Beaches, Restaurant and Activities. As an extra tip, don't forget to visit the Cathedral of Santa Maria of Palma, a unique Cathedral from the 14th century.

If not the most beautiful beach in the world, Calo des Moro is definitely in the top 5. As everything has it's price, to get to the beach a 15 minutes hiking on a narrow trail has to be done. But that is really cheap knowing that in the end, you will arrive in paradise.

Playa de S'Amarador is white sand beach that is part of the protected Mondrago National Park. ’Amarador is 145 metres long and 50 metres wide with fine, white sand and clear, shallow water. If you wish to visit the equally beautiful neighbouring beach of Cala Mondrago, you can follow the 300-400 metre long mountain path that connects the two.

A large, beautiful beach that is family friendly. At nearly 525 metres long, this white sand beach is full of water sports and activities including paddle boats, kayaks, and stand up paddle boarding. The water is clear and clean, with some shallow rocks to watch out for whether you are walking or swimming

Cala Santanyí is located about 10 kilometres from the charming village of Santanyi. The 130-metre long beach is made up of fine, white sand and the waters are clear and calm, perfect for families as well as for good snorkelling. During the summer Cala Santanyí can be really busy, so to find a good spot would be a good idea early in the morning.

Definitely the best restaurant in Cala Figuera. With a rich menu which includes delicious fishes, a big range of different tapas. But the main dish in L'Arcada is the Paella, as you can see in the picture, it looks delicious.

Although you can find delicious food at Na Foradada Restaurant the main "dish" there is the view. If you get a table there(what is not easy), you will be sitting beside a mountain beside the sea. A good tip is you order your food before your after the sunset because will be impossible for you care about food when you are facing the most beautiful sunset you ever saw.

If you are looking for a place to relax and have delicious portions, including a huge range os tapas, Ombu is the place you are looking for. Located in the center of Palma beside of the Plaza de la Reina fountain, makes it a magnet for visitors and the outdoor tables are often full.

Moltabarra Bar de Tapas is a typical Spanish bar. With an old school decoration, the place is very cozy and simple. The perfect place for a happy hour. Moltabarra bar is even famous in Palma, the bar was transformed in the Joan's Bar from the UK series the Mallorca Files.

A lovely way to practice some sports on the beach. You can rent the Stand-Up Paddle and kayaks for one, two, or whatever amount of hours you want, even for the full day. You also don't have to worry about you have any experience, you can a quick explanation with very usual tips, or get a 15 min course. And then jump into the water when you will truly learn.

If a kayak is too small for you, you can have a boat. There are a lot of different boat trips, you can go in the morning to see dolphins, in the evening to enjoy the sunset from a unique view and many more options. There is also the possibility to rent a boat just for you and your family, there are boats for 5 up to 20 people.

Mallorca has two mountain ranges - Serra de Tramuntana and Sierra de Llevant - with more than five summits over 1,000 meters high. There have been guided mountain tours for over three decades, with increasing popularity over the last number of years. Nowadays there is something for everyone. Depending on the level of skill, one can opt for a leisurely mountain walk, suitable for children as well, a rigorous hike for more advanced hikers, or a challenging climb which one should only attempt with suitable equipment and a mountain guide who knows the terrain.

The Lighthouse of Cap de Formentor is a beautiful place and has an incredible view at any time of the day. But the sunset in there is probably the most beautiful you will ever see in your life. On one side there are huge mountains and on the other one the sun saying goodbye to the ocean, the perfect combination.
